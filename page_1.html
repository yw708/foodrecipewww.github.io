Hot Pot 火锅

Hot pot is a traditional winter dish that brings family and friends together around a steaming pot of broth. Everyone cooks ingredients at the table and shares food, symbolizing unity, warmth, and harmony.

Hot pot is popular in northern and southwestern China, especially in Sichuan and Chongqing, where spicy broth helps people stay warm during the cold months.

Ingredients:

  • Broth: chicken or beef soup base, Sichuan spicy paste, or mushroom soup
  • Meats: thinly sliced beef, lamb, or chicken
  • Vegetables: napa cabbage, spinach, mushrooms, lotus root
  • Other: tofu, noodles, fish balls, shrimp, dipping sauces

How to Make Hot Pot:

  • 1. Prepare a pot of broth — you can use chicken, beef, or spicy Sichuan soup base.
  • 2. Slice meat thinly (beef, lamb, or chicken) and wash vegetables (bok choy, mushrooms, spinach).
  • 3. Arrange tofu, noodles, and dipping sauces on the table.
  • 4. Heat the broth until boiling, then add ingredients to cook as you eat.
  • 5. Dip cooked food in sesame or chili sauce and enjoy!
