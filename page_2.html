Dumplings 饺子

Dumplings are one of the most symbolic Chinese winter dishes, especially during the Lunar New Year and the Winter Solstice Festival. Their shape resembles ancient Chinese gold ingots, symbolizing wealth and good fortune for the new year.

Families gather to make dumplings together, representing togetherness and reunion in the coldest season.

Ingredients:

  • Dumpling wrappers
  • Minced pork or beef
  • Chopped napa cabbage or chives
  • Soy sauce, sesame oil, salt, pepper, and ginger

How to Make Dumplings:

  • 1. Mix minced pork, chopped cabbage, soy sauce, and sesame oil to make the filling.
  • 2. Place a small spoon of filling in a dumpling wrapper.
  • 3. Fold and seal the edges tightly with your fingers.
  • 4. Boil water, add dumplings, and cook until they float.
  • 5. Serve with soy sauce, vinegar, and chili oil for dipping.
