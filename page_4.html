Winter Melon Pork Rib Soup 冬瓜仔排汤

Winter Melon Pork Rib Soup is a classic Cantonese-style soup that symbolizes balance and health. In southern China, families often cook it in winter because it helps clear body heat while keeping the body nourished and strong.

Winter melon is known for its cooling effect, while pork ribs provide protein and warmth — making this soup a perfect harmony of yin and yang in Chinese dietary philosophy.

Ingredients:

  • 500g pork ribs
  • 400g winter melon (peeled and cubed)
  • 3 slices of ginger
  • 8 cups water
  • Salt and white pepper to taste

How to Make Winter Melon Pork Rib Soup:

  • 1. Wash and cut 500g of pork ribs into pieces. Blanch in boiling water for 3 minutes and drain.
  • 2. Peel and cut 400g of winter melon into cubes.
  • 3. Put ribs into a pot with 1.5L of water, add ginger slices and simmer for 40 minutes.
  • 4. Add winter melon pieces and continue cooking for 20 minutes until soft.
  • 5. Season with salt and a bit of white pepper before serving.
