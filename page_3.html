Congee 粥

Congee is a soft rice porridge that warms the stomach and nourishes the body. In traditional Chinese medicine, it’s seen as a healing dish that helps balance the body’s energy during cold seasons.

Congee is especially common during early winter mornings or for people recovering from illness. It represents gentleness, care, and nourishment.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup white rice
  • 6 cups water or chicken broth
  • Optional: shredded chicken, preserved egg, or fish
  • Salt and sesame oil to taste

How to Make Congee:

  • 1. Wash 1 cup of rice and soak for 30 minutes.
  • 2. Add rice to 6 cups of water or broth.
  • 3. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 1–1.5 hours until creamy.
  • 4. Add ingredients like shredded chicken, fish, or preserved egg if desired.
  • 5. Season with salt and a little sesame oil before serving.
